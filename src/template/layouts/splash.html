Fast and simple Node.js logger

Pino - extremely fast Node.js logger.
It also includes a shell utility to pretty-print its log files

$ npm install pino --save

What is Pino?

Pino is the fastest logger for Node.js. Pino logs in JSON, and it is API-compatible with Bunyan.

Several applications have been ported to Pino from Bunyan or Winston and they experienced a 20% to 100% throughput increase.
